# Privacy Policy
**The Lost Atlas of Mexico**  
Last updated: [February 25th, 2026]

## Overview

The Lost Atlas of Mexico respects your privacy.

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Information Collection

The Lost Atlas of Mexico:

- Does not require user accounts
- Does not collect personal data
- Does not track user activity
- Does not use analytics services
- Does not include third-party advertising
- Does not connect to the internet

All content and interactions occur entirely on the user’s device.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for educational purposes and is suitable for students.  
Since no personal data is collected, the app complies with child privacy protection standards, including COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act).

## Data Storage

All app data remains locally on the user’s device.  
No information is transmitted externally.

## Third-Party Services

The Lost Atlas of Mexico does not integrate with third-party services, SDKs, or external APIs.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If future updates introduce features that require data collection or internet connectivity, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
